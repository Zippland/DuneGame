Game Instructions

Welcome to the Dune Style Shooting Game!

Instructions:

  • Click on UFOs to shoot them down and earn points.
  • Avoid the sandworm when it appears.
  • Use the shield to protect yourself by clicking the shield button when you have shields available.
  • Beware of sandstorms that can affect UFO movement.
  • If a UFO passes the right edge of the canvas, you lose a life. Lose 3 lives and the game is over.
  • Shoot down 30 UFOs to activate the sandworm button. The sandworm will clear the screen of UFOs.
  • Every 50 UFOs shot down grants an extra life.
